Bolognese

Description

It does not take a lot of ingredients (or a lot of money) to make a classic Bolognese recipe. What it does take, though, is patience for the sauce to achieve the ideal authentic texture. You are going to go low and slowâ€”it will take around 3 hours, but most of that cook time is hands-off.

Ingredients

  • bolognese sauce
  • Pasta
  • Salted Boiled Water

Steps

  1. pour sauce into pan and raise the heat.
  2. boil some water and put the pasta inside, and cook till el dente, it should say on the box how long that takes.
  3. Mix the pasta and sauce together, and add some pasta water.
  4. Lastly, plate the pasta with some flair.
