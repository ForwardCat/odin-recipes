Lasagna

Description

This is the recipe for the lasagna, a staple of Italian cusine and an absolutely irresistable dish if done right!

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it is make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of Bolognese Sauce
  5. Repeat for 2-3 more layers
